University of Richmond Clubs Learn about UR's amazing organizations!

ACM, Association of Computer Machinery

The best club on UR's campus!! This is URs one and only Computer Science Club! Anyone who is interested in learning coding and getting involved in a hands on project come check us out!

Young Life

Young Life is a Christian organization that puts on weekly club meetings! Club is a fun chance to hang out with friends or make some new ones! Come sing songs, play a game, and listen to an interesting talk.

Kairos

Come tackle life's deepest questions by joining the Kairos Community!

MSA, Muslim Students' Association

Ever been curious about Islam? Come be a part of our supportive, enriching, and educational organization!

Residence Life

Come join the residence life staff next year! Applicatons open in January! In the meantime, check out some fun events going on by the RAs in your area!

Frisbee

Athletic? Non-athletic? Come out and see if frisbee is right for you! We are fun, loving group of people who enjoy tossing around a frisbee on the occassion.

Youth Life

Love teaching kids and making an impact in the lives of others? Well come check out Youth Life! We serve at a variety of schools across the Richmond area and building life-changing relationships and mentorships with students of all ages and backgrounds.

Osmosis Magazine

Have a passion for writing, science, health care, or any combination of these? Well, come write or be a part of our amazing Osmosis Magazine staff and support.

Hillel

This is a Jewish community group on campus to allow students the opportunity to enhance their identities and develop strong relationships.
